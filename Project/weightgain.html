Summary

You can expect to see initial improvements in strength and endurance within the first 2-3 weeks of bodyweight exercises. Noticeable muscle growth and changes in body composition typically start around 4-6 weeks, provided you're consistent with your workouts and nutrition. Significant results, including muscle definition and weight gain, usually appear after 8 weeks of consistent effort. Keep in mind that individual progress can vary depending on factors like workout intensity, diet, and recovery.
