Summary

Maintaining fitness can show results within a few days to a week of consistent exercise, with improved energy levels, endurance, and mood. After 2-3 weeks, you may notice better strength and stamina. Long-term maintenance of fitness requires regular exercise, a balanced diet, and proper rest to keep your body in peak condition. As long as you stay consistent, the positive effects of fitness maintenance will be sustained.
