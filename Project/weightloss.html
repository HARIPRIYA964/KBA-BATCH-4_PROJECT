Summary

For weight loss, noticeable results typically begin within 2-4 weeks of consistent exercise and a calorie deficit diet. In the first few weeks, you may lose water weight, followed by gradual fat loss. Significant changes in body composition and visible results often become more apparent after 6-8 weeks, depending on factors like your starting point, workout intensity, and adherence to a healthy diet. Everyone's progress is different, so consistency is key.
